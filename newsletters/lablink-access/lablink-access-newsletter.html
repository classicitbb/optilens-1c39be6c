How to access LabLink

1. Go to classicvisions.net.

2. Open the Professionals menu.

3. Choose Online Ordering Portal or Order Tracking.

LabLink will load inside the Classic Visions website, below our header and above our footer. If your team prefers a separate browser tab, use the small Open LabLink link under the embedded portal.

Please continue using your existing LabLink credentials. If you need help accessing your account, contact the Classic Visions team.
